DEI - HKU Winter Exchange - December 2018 - January 2019



DEI-HKU Winter Experience program entitled “Sustainable Way of Life by Skill Development through Agriculture, Textiles and Yoga” was conducted in Dayalbagh from December 28, 2018 till January 2019. There were 9 students along with 1 teacher of HKU, who attended this program to broach this bilateral student exchange program. The major activities such as working in the Agricultural fields, doing Yoga and textile work were performed by the students together from both HKU and DEI, leading to the exchange of ideas on their understanding and perception of Dayalbagh through the first-person experience. In totality, the trip was informative and a great learning opportunity for the young and brilliant students of both the institutes.
