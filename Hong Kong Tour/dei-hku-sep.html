Sustainable Way of Life by Skill-Development through Agriculture, Textiles and Yoga

The sustainable development and on how it should be interpreted for action through Skill-Developmnent by linking our heritages as well as by giving Indian craft its age-old power. As the largest craft concentration in the world, scholars are making a massive contribution to the search for a more socially just, equitable, sustainable and inclusive planet. Ath the end of the day, there are only two tasks that truly matter are caring for each other and caring for the earth.

The stress levels are monitoring and efficiency going down to counteract these negatives and to regenerate oneself is Yoga with meditation which is the mantra of the day. Handmade in India for the world was a dream till now, with the collaborative work being taken up, the students, researchers, faculties, outreach centres, adapting traditional skills to produce new products. This adaptation is accompalished in many areas including fashion, home furnishings, entrepreneurship, Agriculture and Dairy. Various strategies are being devised and developed from which students are benefitting.
