Introduction

Introducing myself, I am Arshita Satsangee, a first year student pursuing my Major studies in Psychology and English from Dayalbagh Educational Institute, Agra, India.
Being a national Table Tennis player and having an avid interest in various fields like adventure sports and travelling, my areas of interest also revolve
around History, be it visiting historical monuments, reading historical books or watching Historical stage shows.For the next few years from now, I have kept my options open considering getting into the field of Law or pursuing a Master's in psychology.
The skill sets which I would like to acquire apart from excelling in academics are pottery-making and learning about the cultural diversities and cuisines.

I am a backpacker and a gypsy soul looking to explore different landscapes. I live by the saying 'Carpe Diem'-Seize the Day (making the most of the day and most of life).
The 3 precepts I live by are Adhyayn ( ongoing learning and education). Seva ( inculcating the spirit of selfless service and contributing to the community at large). Vyayam ( a sound mind in a healthy body because only when we remain fit , we can serve others).
