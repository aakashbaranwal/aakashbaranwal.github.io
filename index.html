I was a member of the 10 member team which went from Dayalbagh Educational Institute(Agra, India) to Hong Kong University (Hong Kong, China) for a Student Exchange Program. We were there for around 14 days during which we made educational trips to few places such as Sheung Wan, Tai O, Long Valley, Pok Fu Lam and few more. We also had an opportunity to enjoy ourseleves at Ocean Park on one fine and lovely sunday morning going on Roller Coasters, Water Rides, Free Fall (Oh the fear of being dropped from a height of over 200 Feet and then getting stopped all together literally kills the person!!) and so on.

Any event in Hong Kong happens and people would know about it on my website, newsletter subscription and so on are some of the few things which was there.
